Siva Sankar Pandit

Siva Sankar Pandit was a great Indian philosopher and scholar. He was born in 1840 in Varanasi, India. He was a master of Sanskrit and studied many other languages as well. He was also a prolific writer and produced many works on philosophy, religion, and literature.

Pandit was a staunch advocate of Hindu revivalism and was a strong critic of Western influences. He was also a champion of women's rights and social reform. He died in 1922 in Varanasi.
